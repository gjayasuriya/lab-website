Understanding Seed Traits, Germination & Plant Regeneration

We study seed ecology, dormancy mechanisms, and plant responses to environmental change, especially in tropical ecosystems.

About the Lab

Our laboratory focuses on plant regeneration ecology, seed functional traits, and germination strategies. We aim to understand how environmental factors shape plant recruitment and ecosystem dynamics.

Research Themes

  • Seed dormancy and germination ecology
  • Functional seed traits and plant strategies
  • Effects of climate and salinity on regeneration
  • Invasive species and forest regeneration
  • Mangrove community ecology
  • Medicinal plants of Sri Lanka
